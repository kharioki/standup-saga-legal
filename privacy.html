Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2025

StandUp Saga (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, display name, and authentication ID
  • Usage Data: Updates you generate and submit
  • Device Information: Device ID, OS version, app version (non-personal)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable core app features like creating and viewing standup updates
  • To personalize your experience and save your teams/parties
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To provide support and communicate important updates

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only shared with Firebase services (authentication, Firestore, etc.) for app functionality.

4. Data Retention

We retain user-generated data (updates, teams) as long as your account exists or until you delete it. You may delete your account at any time from the app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

StandUp Saga uses Firebase (by Google) and RevenueCat to manage authentication, storage, and subscriptions. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data or export your information. Contact us at: support@standupsaga.app

7. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Major changes will be communicated via app update or in-app notification.

Thank you for using StandUp Saga!
