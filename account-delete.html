Account and Data Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from StandUp Saga, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email us at tony.kharioki@gmail.com with the subject “Delete My Account”.
  2. Include your username or the email used in the app.
  3. We’ll delete your data and confirm within 7 business days.

If you're logged into the app, you can also go to Settings > Delete My Account.
